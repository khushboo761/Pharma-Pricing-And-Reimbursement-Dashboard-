# Pharma Pricing & Reimbursement Dashboard

An interactive web dashboard analyzing Medicare Part D drug pricing, brand vs generic spending patterns, and price spike detection across therapy categories (2019–2022).

🔗 **Live Dashboard**: [View Here](https://effulgent-babka-9f4c3d.netlify.app)

## Overview

This project explores how drug pricing and reimbursement trends vary across therapy categories, drug types, and years using CMS Medicare Part D public data. It is designed to surface actionable insights relevant to formulary management, pricing strategy, and reimbursement risk — core areas in pharma commercial analytics.

## Key Features

- **Cost per Unit Trend** — Track spending trajectory for top drugs over time
- **Brand vs Generic Spending** — Compare reimbursement exposure by drug type
- **Spend by Therapy Category** — Identify which categories drive the highest spend
- **Price Change Heatmap** — Spot drugs exceeding a custom price spike threshold
- **Price vs Beneficiary Scatter** — Surface high-price or high-volume outlier drugs
- **Spike Risk by Category** — See which therapy areas carry the most pricing volatility

## Filters & Interactivity

- Drug category selector
- Year range (2019–2022)
- Drug type toggle (All / Brand / Generic)
- Adjustable price spike threshold %

- Coverage: 2019–2022

## Business Context

Medicare Part D reimbursement data is widely used in pharma commercial analytics for:
- Formulary positioning and access strategy
- Pricing benchmarking across therapy areas
- Identifying at-risk drugs with high price volatility

This project simulates the kind of analysis performed at firms like **ZS Associates, Fractal, PharmaAce, and ProcDNA** in their life sciences analytics practice.
